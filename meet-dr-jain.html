Meet Dr. Jain!

Dr. Mamta Jain is a board certified physician with over 20 years of experience in internal medicine. Her mission is to provide leading edge medical care to help you live a healthier, more enjoyable life.

Dr. Jain graduated from medical school in India, finishing her residency in internal medicine in New York and her fellowship in critical care medicine at Albert Einstein College of Medicine in 2008. Dr. Jain has active privileges at Memorial Hermann Hospital of the Woodlands and St. Luke's The Woodlands Hospital. Dr. Jain has extensive experience in inpatient and outpatient diagnostic and therapeutic medical management.

Dr. Jain is a member of the American College of Physicians (ACP), the Texas Medical Association (TMA), and the Harris County Medical Society (HCMS).

Outside of patient care, Dr. Jain enjoys many principles of healthy living. Some of her favorite activities are yoga, meditation, sports, and cooking nutritious cuisine.

Married to a devoted interventional cardiologist and herself a devoted mother of 4 children, Dr. Jain enjoys the rigors of both private practice and homemaking and deeply values the families of her patients.
