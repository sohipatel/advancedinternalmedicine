Telemedicine Information:

If this is an emergency, call 911.
Your insurance will be billed for this visit. You are responsible for your copay/coinsurance/deductibles as required by your insurance plan.
Your telemedicine provider may request an in-person follow-up visit before a diagnosis can be made, and care will be provided if necessary. Telemedicine charges still apply.
Your telemedicine session is private.

  • Advantages of telemedicine:
    • No transportation time or costs
    • No need to take time off of work
    • Eliminate child or elder care issues
    • On-demand options
    • Less chance of catching a new illness
    • Less time in the waiting room
    • Better health overall

    • Allergies
    • Arthritic pain
    • Asthma
    • Bladder infections
    • Bronchitis
    • Colds and flu
    • Diarrhea
    • Infections
    • Lab result reviews
