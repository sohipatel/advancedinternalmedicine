Welcome to Advanced Internal Medicine!

Dr. Mamta Jain is a board-certified physician in internal medicine. She provides cutting-edge medical care that leads to a healthier and more enjoyable life for her patients. Dr. Jain is committed to delivering compassionate care.
In 2010, Dr. Mamta Jain established Advanced Internal Medicine of the Woodlands to serve the residents of Montgomery County. Her focus is providing effective patient care that treats diseases and promotes health.
Dr. Jain's patient care philosophy is built on the foundation of working in collaboration with patients and their families. She adopts a patient care strategy that encompasses prevention, early detection, comprehensive evaluation, and a multidisciplinary approach to therapy.
